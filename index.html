CISSP is an advanced-level certification from ISC2, covering eight security domains. It's one of the most frequently required credentials for senior security roles. The certification body requires a minimum of 5 years of paid security experience before you earn it. If you're earlier in your career, Security+ or CEH is the right place to start.
